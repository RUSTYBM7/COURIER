Admin Manual

Complete guide to the Airpak Express Admin Portal

Getting Started

Welcome to the Airpak Express Admin Portal. This system gives you complete control over all logistics operations. Below is a comprehensive guide to every feature.

Quick Start

  1. Review the Dashboard for real-time KPIs and alerts
  2. Use Client Management to onboard new clients
  3. Create shipments via Parcel Management → Create Parcel
  4. Track all shipments in Live Progress
  5. Use AI Logistics Centre for route optimisation and predictions

Email Automation

The Email Automation Centre has 6 pre-configured AI-powered sequences:

  • Welcome Email: 3-email onboarding sequence for new clients
  • Newsletter: Monthly industry updates sent automatically
  • Status Updates: Auto-sent when any shipment changes status
  • Delay Alerts: Proactive notification for any delay or exception
  • Invoice Reminders: Automated follow-ups at 7, 14, 30 days
  • SLA Escalation: Auto-escalates critical breaches to management

Role Management

You are the Super Admin. You can invite up to 2 additional admin accounts (max 3 total). Admins have full access. You can also create Manager roles for specific functions (Transit Manager, Warehouse Manager, Client Relations Manager, etc.). Managers have limited access based on their assigned permissions.

AI Features

The AI Logistics Centre can: predict SLA breaches before they happen, automatically reroute delayed shipments, classify customs HS codes, optimise routes for cost and speed, answer natural language queries about any shipment or client, and generate reports on demand.

Payment & Billing

The system supports GBP payments. Use Invoice Generator to create client invoices. Payment Verification handles pending transactions. All payments are recorded in Payment Records. Crypto payments are accepted — clients can buy crypto via the integrated Changelly widget on the payment page.

Workflow Automation

Automate parcel creation from orders, auto-send status notifications, AI reroutes on delay >6h, and monthly auto-invoicing. All automations can be toggled on/off in the Workflow Automation panel.

Support

For technical support, contact the development team. All actions are logged in Audit Logs for compliance and accountability.
